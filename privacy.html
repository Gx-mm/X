Privacy Policy – GrowX
Last Updated: 2026

Introduction

Welcome to GrowX. Your privacy and security are important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how GrowX collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our services.

By using GrowX services, you agree to the terms mentioned in this policy.

Information We Collect

When you create an account on GrowX, we may collect the following information:

  • Username
  • Email Address
  • Mobile Number
  • Order Details
  • Payment Details (UTR / Transaction ID)

This information is collected only to provide and manage our services properly.

We never ask for your social media passwords. All services are performed using publicly available links only.

How We Use Your Information

  • Creating and managing your account
  • Processing your orders
  • Verifying payments
  • Providing customer support
  • Improving our platform and services

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties.

Your information is used strictly for service-related purposes and not for any unauthorized activities.

Data Protection

GrowX uses secure systems to protect your information. Your data is stored safely and used only for service purposes.

However, users are responsible for keeping their login credentials secure.

We do not misuse or expose user data under any circumstances.

Service Policy

GrowX provides social media marketing services such as:

  • Followers
  • Likes
  • Views
  • Story Views
  • Other social media engagement services

GrowX reserves the right to suspend or block accounts that violate these rules.

Payment Policy

All payments made on GrowX must be submitted with a valid UTR / TXN ID.

  • Each payment must have a unique UTR / Transaction ID
  • The same UTR / TXN ID cannot be submitted more than once

Duplicate UTR submission may cause payment failure due to server verification issues.

Users must ensure correct payment details before submitting. GrowX is not responsible for incorrect payment submissions.

Refund Policy

  • If an order cannot be completed by the system
  • If the service becomes unavailable after payment
  • If there is a verified technical error on the platform

Refunds will be added to the GrowX account balance only.

No refund will be provided once an order is successfully completed.

Order Policy

  • Account must be public
  • Correct link must be provided
  • Correct order quantity must be selected

GrowX is not responsible for incorrect links, private accounts, or wrong order details provided by users.

Account Policy

Users must not create multiple accounts for misuse of offers or bonuses.

GrowX reserves the right to suspend or terminate accounts involved in fraud or misuse without prior notice.

Changes to This Policy

GrowX may update this Privacy Policy at any time. Users are advised to check this page periodically for updates.
